Who Are We ?

When I say 'We,' I'm referring to a lone individual. A humble developer navigating the expansive landscapes of technology. My journey is propelled by a fervor for constructing solutions, frequently encountering captivating ideas that may not have a monumental purpose but endure because they align with my hobbies and contribute to my continuous learning. Every so often, amidst these explorations, I stumble upon ideas that turn out to be genuinely beneficial, not only to myself but potentially to others as well.

Why Byte Picks Exists ?

Byte Picks didn't materialize on a whim, it was born out of a genuine need. In a digital landscape drowning in content (especially on Youtube), I envisioned a platform that stands out by prioritizing quality over quantity. Byte Picks strives to be a sanctuary for tech enthusiasts, providing a curated, relevant, and insightful collection without the overwhelming noise.

Behind the Name: Byte Picks

Wondering why 'Byte Picks'? It's not as complicated as it might sound. 'Byte' symbolizes the digital realm, while 'Picks' represents our carefully selected, top-tier content. Together, these words encapsulate our commitment to delivering quality tech insights, one pick at a time.

How We Developed Byte Picks ?

If you're curious about the inner workings, I strongly encourage you to explore our Behind the Scenes page. There, you can delve into the impressive efforts of our talented engineers (mainly me) who poured their skills into creating this service for everyday folks, free of charge or burden.

Why Open Source and Free ?

Byte Picks is proudly open source and free. Why? It's rooted in a personal belief, an aspiration for an internet that's free from spyware and censorship. There's no grand plan to monetize, it's a labor of love that I'm excited to share with the world.

Can You Contribute ?

Without a doubt! Byte Picks resides on GitHub, and I welcome contributions from fellow tech enthusiasts. Join me in shaping and enhancing Byte Picks into something even more remarkable!
